📖 Our Story

About InAmigos Foundation

A youth-driven movement built on compassion, integrity, and collective action for lasting social change.

Who We Are

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation was born from the belief that a more equal society is possible when people act together. Operating from Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, we address India's most pressing social issues — poverty, lack of education, gender inequality, animal suffering, and environmental degradation.

We are one of India's fastest-growing youth-led NGOs, uniting volunteers, professionals, students, and community leaders toward a single purpose: lasting, dignified change for those who need it most. Through our six flagship projects, we have reached 50,000+ beneficiaries across 28 states.

🎯 Our Vision

To build an inclusive society where every individual has the opportunity to learn, grow, and achieve their full potential regardless of their background.

🚀 Our Mission

To uplift underprivileged communities by providing access to education, skill development, healthcare awareness, environmental sustainability, and social support programs.

🌟 Our Goal

Expand city by city, build a strong volunteer base, and create lasting impact for millions across India through sustainable community programs.

Our Works & Projects

Each initiative addresses a distinct social challenge — together creating a complete ecosystem of upliftment.

Project Seva

Serving humanity with compassion — food distribution drives, clothing donations, essential supplies, and community support initiatives. Our volunteers run regular campaigns to ensure basic necessities reach those who need them most.

🫶 Food & Clothing Drives

Project Bachpanshala

Education is the foundation of a brighter future. Supporting underprivileged children through educational assistance, school supplies, digital literacy awareness, and mentorship opportunities to ensure every child gets quality education.

🎓 Education for Every Child

Project Udaan

Empowering women for a better future — skill development training, leadership programs, career guidance, entrepreneurship awareness, and personal development workshops to help women gain confidence and independence.

💪 Women's Empowerment

Project Prakriti

Environmental sustainability is a shared responsibility. We organise tree plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns, environmental awareness programs, waste management initiatives, and community participation activities for a greener future.

🌍 Environmental Conservation

Project Jeev

Animals are an important part of our ecosystem and deserve compassion. Project Jeev works on animal rescue initiatives, welfare awareness, regular feeding programs, and community engagement for animal protection across cities.

🐕 Animal Rescue & Care

Project Vikas

Building future leaders — designed for students and young professionals seeking growth. Internship opportunities, skill-building workshops, career guidance sessions, leadership development, and professional networking. 30,000+ interns impacted.

Why This Work Matters

📖 Breaking the Poverty–Education Cycle

Millions of children miss school due to poverty. Project Bachpanshala bridges that gap with free educational support, supplies, and mentorship — ensuring no child is left behind.

👩 Unlocking Women's Potential

Through Project Udaan, women gain the skills and confidence to become economically independent — transforming families and communities from within.

🌱 Healing the Environment

Project Prakriti's tree plantation drives and awareness campaigns restore green cover while educating communities on sustainable living — one sapling at a time.

🍽️ Nourishing the Hungry Project Seva's food drives ensure underprivileged families and the homeless have access to nutritious meals, warm clothing, and essential supplies regularly.
🚀 Building the Leaders of Tomorrow Project Vikas has empowered 30,000+ young individuals with practical internship experience, professional skills, and career confidence — shaping India's future workforce.

❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about InAmigos Foundation.

What is InAmigos Foundation? ▾
InAmigos Foundation is a youth-led Section 8 registered NGO founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla. Operating from Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, we work across 28 states of India through six flagship projects: Seva, Bachpanshala, Udaan, Prakriti, Jeev, and Vikas — transforming lives through education, hunger relief, women empowerment, environmental care, animal welfare, and youth development.
How can I volunteer with InAmigos? ▾
You can volunteer by filling out our volunteer registration form at the link below. Our team will review your application and get back to you. Click the "Volunteer With Us" button on the home page or visit forms.gle/AB4c1hLaDDmtrKGU7 to apply directly.
How can I join the InAmigos team? ▾
To join as a permanent team member, visit inamigosfoundation.org.in/became-volunteer. You can also click the "Join the Team" button on our home page. Team members get opportunities in leadership, project management, social media, events, and more.
How can I donate to InAmigos Foundation? ▾
Click the "Donate Now" button on the top navigation bar. You can scan our UPI QR code or donate directly via Razorpay at rzp.io/l/kWQ87HP. We are 80G and 12A certified — your donations are eligible for tax exemption.
Who can join the internship programs? ▾
Students and young professionals interested in social impact and skill development can apply for Project Vikas internships. We have already trained 30,000+ interns across India. Interns gain real-world experience, certificates, and professional networking opportunities.
What projects does InAmigos run? ▾
InAmigos runs six flagship projects: 🍚 Project Seva (food & clothing), 📚 Project Bachpanshala (education), 🦋 Project Udaan (women's empowerment), 🌳 Project Prakriti (environment), 🐾 Project Jeev (animal welfare), and 🚀 Project Vikas (youth development & internships).
Can I participate remotely? ▾
Yes! Certain activities — including Project Vikas internships, content creation, social media, awareness campaigns, and fundraising drives — can be done remotely. Reach out to us at support@inamigosfoundation.org.in to learn more.
Is InAmigos a registered NGO? ▾
Yes. InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered NGO based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. We hold 80G & 12A tax certifications, CSR-1 registration, NITI Aayog registration, and ISO 9001:2015 certification — ensuring full transparency and accountability.
How are donations used? ▾
Your donations directly fund food distribution drives, school supplies for children, skill development programs for women, tree plantation campaigns, animal welfare activities, and internship infrastructure. We maintain complete transparency in fund utilisation.
